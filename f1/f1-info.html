What is Formula 1?

Formula 1, abbreviated as F1, is the pinnacle of single-seater auto racing, showcasing the latest in technology, innovation, and driver skill.

History

Since its inception in 1950, F1 has evolved into a multi-billion-dollar sport that continually sets new standards in automotive performance.

The Teams and Drivers

From legendary teams like Mercedes-AMG Petronas, Scuderia Ferrari, and Red Bull Racing to new contenders such as McLaren, Racing Bulls, Aston Martin, and Williams, F1 is a showcase of cutting-edge innovation.
