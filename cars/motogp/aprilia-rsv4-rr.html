Aprilia RSV4 RR

Specifications

Production Years 2015 - Present
Engine 999cc V4 Engine
Power 201 HP
Transmission 6-Speed Sequential Manual

Overview

The Aprilia RSV4 RR is a high-performance, race-ready superbike that embodies cutting-edge technology and Italian design excellence. Praised for its agility and dynamic performance on the track, it continues to be a benchmark in superbike racing.

History & Development

Launched in 2015, the RSV4 RR has continuously evolved with rigorous testing and input from professional riders. This relentless pursuit of perfection has made it a dominant force in competitive superbike racing.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Crafted with a lightweight, aggressive chassis and cutting-edge aerodynamic design, the RSV4 RR minimizes drag and maximizes stability, delivering a razor-sharp performance in every twist and turn on the track.

Engine & Performance

Under the hood, the potent 999cc V4 engine churns out 201 horsepower, ensuring explosive acceleration and a responsive throttle. This superbike’s engineering is honed for rapid performance and precision on the race circuit.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Every RSV4 RR is meticulously crafted in Italy with a passion for motorsport. Aprilia’s commitment to precision engineering and innovative design is evident in each machine that leaves the factory.

Mileage & Efficiency

Designed explicitly for the track, the RSV4 RR expertly balances raw power with efficient fuel management, ensuring sustained performance even during high-intensity racing events.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Equipped with advanced rider aids, modern braking systems, and precision-engineered traction control, the RSV4 RR ensures unparalleled safety and driver confidence under extreme racing conditions.

Additional Information

Continuously pushing the boundaries of superbike performance, the Aprilia RSV4 RR remains a symbol of innovation and racing excellence, garnering admiration from racers and enthusiasts around the world.
