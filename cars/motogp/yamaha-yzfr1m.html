Yamaha YZF-R1M

Specifications

Production Years 2020 - Present
Engine 998cc liquid-cooled inline-four
Power Approximately 200 HP
Transmission 6-speed manual
Features High-performance electronics, lightweight frame, advanced aerodynamics

Overview

The Yamaha YZF-R1M is the pinnacle of Yamaha’s superbike lineup—a track-focused variant that fuses race-proven technology with unparalleled performance and cutting-edge design.

History & Development

Launched in 2020, the R1M is the product of extensive development and testing, incorporating feedback from professional riders to create a machine that not only delivers blistering performance but also sets new industry benchmarks.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Engineered with an emphasis on aerodynamic efficiency, the R1M features a state-of-the-art fairing and chassis design that minimize drag while maximizing downforce, ensuring supreme stability at high speeds.

Engine & Performance

The 998cc liquid-cooled inline-four delivers explosive power with razor-sharp throttle response, providing racers with the performance edge required for competitive track environments.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Built in Japan by Yamaha’s elite production teams, every R1M is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, reflecting the brand’s longstanding dedication to precision engineering and high-performance innovation.

Mileage & Efficiency

Despite its race-ready focus, the R1M is designed to balance raw power with efficiency, ensuring sustained track performance over extended sessions without compromise.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Equipped with advanced braking systems, traction control, and rider-centric ergonomics, the R1M offers superior safety features that empower riders to push limits with confidence.

Additional Information

More than just a motorcycle, the Yamaha YZF-R1M represents Yamaha’s commitment to revolutionizing sportbike performance, making it the ultimate machine for professional racers and serious enthusiasts worldwide.
