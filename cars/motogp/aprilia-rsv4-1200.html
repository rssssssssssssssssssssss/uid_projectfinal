Aprilia RSV4 1200

Specifications

Production Years 2018 - Present
Engine 1197cc V4 Engine
Power Approximately 215 HP
Transmission 6-Speed Sequential Manual
Features Advanced electronics, improved cooling systems, enhanced suspension and braking performance

Overview

The Aprilia RSV4 1200 is the pinnacle of superbike engineering, melding raw power with precision control. Designed for both the track and spirited road riding, it pushes the limits of performance and technology.

History & Development

Launched in 2018 as an evolution of the acclaimed RSV4 platform, the RSV4 1200 boasts extensive research and development. Continuous improvements based on rider feedback have enabled it to set new standards in the superbike world.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Featuring a lightweight, agile chassis paired with a meticulously engineered aerodynamic package, the RSV4 1200 minimizes drag and maximizes downforce – delivering extraordinary stability and cornering prowess at high speeds.

Engine & Performance

Powered by a 1197cc V4 engine, the RSV4 1200 develops approximately 215 horsepower. Its sophisticated engine mapping and razor-sharp throttle response provide an exhilarating yet controllable performance that is ideal for competitive racing and dynamic riding.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Every RSV4 1200 is crafted in Italy by Aprilia’s expert teams. Combining advanced manufacturing techniques with traditional craftsmanship, each unit reflects Aprilia’s commitment to quality, innovation, and performance excellence.

Mileage & Efficiency

Engineered to balance aggressive performance with efficiency, the RSV4 1200 offers competitive fuel economy without sacrificing its high-speed credentials, making it well-suited for both endurance and sprint events.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Outfitted with advanced braking systems, state-of-the-art traction control, and rider-assist features, the RSV4 1200 ensures maximum safety. These systems provide enhanced stability and control, empowering riders to push boundaries with confidence.

Additional Information

The Aprilia RSV4 1200 stands as a symbol of innovation and passion in superbike technology. Constantly evolving and revered by racers and enthusiasts alike, it continues to set benchmarks in performance, design, and rider experience.
