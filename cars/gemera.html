Koenigsegg Gemera

Specifications

Production Years 2023 - Present
Engine 2.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-3 + Hybrid
Power 1,700 HP (combined)
Transmission Koenigsegg Direct Drive (KDD)
Features All-wheel drive, four-seat hypercar, advanced hybrid powertrain

Overview

The Koenigsegg Gemera is the world's first four-seater hypercar, blending extreme performance with practicality. It delivers an astonishing 1,700 horsepower while offering luxury and space for four passengers.

History & Development

The Gemera was introduced in 2020 as part of Koenigsegg's vision for high-performance hybrid vehicles. It represents a revolutionary step in automotive engineering, combining power, efficiency, and practicality.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Built on a lightweight carbon-fiber monocoque, the Gemera offers maximum rigidity while keeping weight low. Its aerodynamic design optimizes both stability and speed, ensuring a thrilling driving experience.

Engine & Hybrid Powertrain

The Gemera is powered by a twin-turbo 2.0L three-cylinder engine, paired with three electric motors, producing a combined 1,700 HP. This hybrid system provides instant torque and enhanced efficiency.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Produced in limited numbers, the Gemera is meticulously handcrafted with Koenigsegg’s cutting-edge technology. Each unit undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure top-tier performance and exclusivity.

Mileage & Efficiency

Despite its hypercar status, the Gemera achieves remarkable fuel efficiency with its hybrid powertrain, making it one of the most sustainable high-performance cars on the market.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Equipped with advanced stability control, high-performance brakes, and reinforced safety features, the Gemera ensures driver and passenger security without compromising performance.

Additional Information

The Gemera redefines what a hypercar can be. With a luxurious, high-tech interior and revolutionary performance, it bridges the gap between everyday usability and extreme speed.
