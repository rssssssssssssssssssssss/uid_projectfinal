Toyota Camry Cup

Specifications

Production Years 2018 - Present
Engine 2.5L Turbocharged Engine
Power Approximately 350 HP
Transmission 6-Speed Sequential Gearbox
Features Optimized for oval racing, durable chassis, competitive performance

Overview

The Toyota Camry Cup is engineered exclusively for oval racing, merging the dependable Camry platform with race-tuned performance and aerodynamic precision.

History & Development

Since its inception in 2018, the Camry Cup has evolved through extensive testing, emerging as a stalwart in oval racing with continuous performance enhancements.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Featuring a reinforced chassis and optimized aerodynamic design, the Camry Cup maximizes stability and minimizes drag on high-speed oval tracks.

Engine & Performance

Its 2.5L turbocharged engine delivers a consistent power band, while the sequential gearbox allows for rapid shifts, making it ideal for the fast-paced demands of oval racing.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Assembled by Toyota’s specialized motorsport division, each Camry Cup is built to exacting standards ensuring durability, reliability, and top-tier performance on the track.

Mileage & Efficiency

Engineered for endurance, it delivers a robust performance over extended races while maintaining an efficient balance between power and fuel consumption.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Equipped with cutting-edge safety systems and driver aids, the Camry Cup provides superb control and protection during the rigorous conditions of oval racing.

Additional Information

Forged for champions, the Toyota Camry Cup continues to set benchmarks in oval racing, with relentless innovation and a commitment to performance excellence.
