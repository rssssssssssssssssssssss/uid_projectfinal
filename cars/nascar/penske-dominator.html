Overview

The Penske Dominator is engineered to dominate the racetrack with its aggressive styling and unrivaled performance, embodying the spirit of NASCAR at its finest.

History & Development

From its debut in 2020, the Dominator has pushed the boundaries of innovation with continuous improvements and rigorous testing to excel in competitive NASCAR events.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Its design is optimized for minimal drag and maximum downforce, ensuring exceptional stability and cornering ability even at extreme speeds.

Engine & Performance

Powered by a robust V8 engine, the Dominator delivers explosive acceleration and consistent performance that sets a new standard in its class.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Each Dominator is built by Team Penske’s expert crew, reflecting meticulous craftsmanship and a legacy of excellence in NASCAR engineering.

Mileage & Efficiency

Despite its immense power, the Dominator is designed to balance performance with fuel efficiency, allowing sustained high-speed performance during long races.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Equipped with advanced braking and stability systems, it provides drivers with the assurance and control needed to push its limits safely.

Additional Information

Constantly evolving and always competitive, the Penske Dominator is a true icon in NASCAR, symbolizing innovation, power, and a relentless pursuit of racing excellence.
