Toyota Supra Xfinity

Specifications

Production Years 2021 - Present
Engine 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six
Power Approximately 450 HP
Transmission 6-Speed Sequential Gearbox
Features Race-tuned suspension, aerodynamic enhancements, lightweight construction

Overview

The Toyota Supra Xfinity is a high-performance race car derived from the iconic Supra lineage—adapted and optimized for the competitive demands of the Xfinity series.

History & Development

Introduced in 2021, the Supra Xfinity underwent rigorous testing and modifications to seamlessly blend streetcar DNA with professional race engineering.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Its lightweight chassis coupled with aerodynamic bodywork maximizes downforce, ensuring superb handling and stability in high-speed races.

Engine & Performance

Under the hood, a turbocharged 3.0L inline-six delivers impressive power and responsiveness, making every lap a thrilling experience.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Built by Toyota’s dedicated motorsport team, each Supra Xfinity is crafted with attention to precision and a passion for racing excellence.

Mileage & Efficiency

Engineered for endurance, it strikes an optimal balance between aggressive power output and fuel efficiency over prolonged races.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Modern safety features and driver-assistance technologies help maintain control and safety even at the highest competitive speeds.

Additional Information

The Toyota Supra Xfinity stands as a testament to innovation and performance, continuously setting new benchmarks in the world of professional racing.
