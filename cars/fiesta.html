Ford Fiesta

Specifications

Production Years 1976 - Present
Engine Options 1.0L EcoBoost, 1.5L Diesel
Power 75 - 200 HP
Transmission 5-speed Manual, 6-speed Automatic
Key Features Agile handling, fuel efficiency, modern infotainment

Overview

The Ford Fiesta is a compact supermini celebrated for its nimble handling and stylish design. It has become a favorite in urban environments due to its affordability and efficient performance.

History

Introduced in 1976, the Fiesta has seen multiple generations of updates, each enhancing its efficiency and design. Its evolution represents decades of innovation in the compact car segment.

Chassis & Design

Built on a lightweight platform, the Fiesta’s design is optimized for maneuverability and comfort. Its compact dimensions and aerodynamic styling contribute to its dynamic performance.

Engine & Performance

Offering a range of engines, including the turbocharged 1.0L EcoBoost, the Fiesta strikes a balance between spirited performance and excellent fuel economy—ideal for city driving and longer journeys alike.

Manufacturing & Global Reach

Manufactured by Ford for over four decades, the Fiesta has achieved global success. Its continuous updates and commitment to quality have made it a benchmark in the supermini category.

Mileage & Efficiency

Renowned for its fuel efficiency, the Fiesta offers impressive mileage figures, making it an economical choice without compromising on driving pleasure.

Safety & Technology

Modern incarnations of the Fiesta include advanced safety features—airbags, ABS, and electronic stability control—along with state-of-the-art infotainment systems that keep drivers connected.

Additional Information

With its stylish exterior and practical interior, the Ford Fiesta continues to captivate drivers worldwide by delivering a unique blend of performance, efficiency, and reliability.
