Ferrari SF1000

Specifications

Production Years 2023 - Present
Engine 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbo Hybrid with enhanced electric motor
Power 1020 HP
Transmission 8-speed dual-clutch automatic
Features Advanced plug-in hybrid system, All-Wheel Drive, cutting-edge aerodynamics

Overview

The Ferrari SF1000 represents the next evolution of hybrid performance, delivering unprecedented power and agility on both road and track.

History & Development

Introduced in 2023, the SF1000 builds on decades of Ferrari expertise, incorporating state-of-the-art hybrid technology and innovative design elements.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Crafted with ultralight materials and an innovative active aerodynamic system, the SF1000 enhances downforce and ensures exceptional cornering precision.

Engine & Hybrid Powertrain

Combining a legendary V8 engine with a cutting-edge hybrid system, the SF1000 delivers explosive performance while maintaining refined efficiency.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Handcrafted in Maranello, Italy, the SF1000 stands as a testament to Ferrari’s commitment to both traditional craftsmanship and modern innovation.

Mileage & Efficiency

Engineered for exhilarating speed and improved fuel economy, the SF1000 maximizes efficiency through its advanced hybrid power unit.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Equipped with Ferrari’s latest safety technology, including adaptive stability control and driver assistance features, the SF1000 ensures a secure yet thrilling drive.

Additional Information

The cockpit features futuristic digital displays and premium materials, creating an immersive and driver-focused experience that is both luxurious and state-of-the-art.
