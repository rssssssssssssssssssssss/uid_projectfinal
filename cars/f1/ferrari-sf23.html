Ferrari SF23

Specifications

Production Year 2023
Engine 1.6L V6 Hybrid Turbocharged
Power Over 1000 HP
Transmission 8-speed sequential
Chassis Carbon-fiber monocoque

Overview

The Ferrari SF23 epitomizes cutting-edge Formula 1 technology, combining breathtaking performance with advanced hybrid systems in a design that is both aggressive and elegant.

History & Development

Part of Ferrari’s storied racing legacy, the SF23 was developed for the 2023 season. It reflects decades of research, innovation, and competitive spirit in the world of F1.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Constructed with a lightweight carbon-fiber monocoque, the SF23 incorporates active aerodynamic features to optimize downforce, reduce drag, and enhance stability at high speeds.

Engine & Hybrid Powertrain

Its hybrid V6 turbo engine delivers over 1000 HP, merging combustion and electric power. This advanced powertrain ensures rapid acceleration and exceptional performance while managing fuel consumption efficiently.

Manufacturing & Innovation

Ferrari’s use of state-of-the-art materials and precision engineering makes the SF23 a marvel of modern automotive technology, setting new benchmarks in both performance and design.

Mileage & Efficiency

While the SF23 is built for extreme performance, its hybrid system also delivers improved fuel efficiency compared to traditional race cars, balancing power with sustainability.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Incorporating race-grade safety features such as advanced braking, stability control, and a reinforced cockpit, the SF23 ensures that performance and driver safety go hand in hand.

Additional Information

The driver-focused interior is minimalistic yet highly functional, offering all critical performance data through digital displays, ensuring the driver remains connected to every aspect of the car's operation.
