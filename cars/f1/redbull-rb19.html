Red Bull RB19

Specifications

Production Years 2023 - Present
Engine 1.6L V6 Turbo Hybrid
Power Approx. 1000 HP
Transmission 8-speed sequential gearbox
Features Next‑gen hybrid technology, enhanced aerodynamics, innovative energy recovery

Overview

The Red Bull RB19 is a state-of-the-art Formula 1 car that combines next‑generation hybrid technology with exceptional aerodynamic design to maximize race performance.

History & Development

Introduced in the 2023 season, the RB19 builds upon Red Bull Racing’s relentless innovation, refining every aspect from powertrain efficiency to chassis dynamics.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Engineered to minimize drag and boost downforce, the RB19’s aerodynamic package coupled with a lightweight chassis delivers outstanding cornering and stability.

Engine & Hybrid Powertrain

The heart of the RB19 lies in its 1.6L V6 turbo hybrid engine, which—combined with advanced energy recovery systems—provides a superb balance of power and efficiency.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Every element of the RB19 is crafted with utmost precision by Red Bull Racing’s expert engineers, empowering the car to meet the extreme demands of modern F1 racing.

Mileage & Efficiency

The RB19’s innovative hybrid systems not only maximize on-track performance but also ensure optimal fuel management and energy recovery for sustained race pace.

Safety & Driver Assistance

With integrated telemetry and advanced safety features, the RB19 supports drivers with real-time insights and critical assistive technologies throughout every race.

Additional Information

Combining relentless research with pioneering engineering, the RB19 stands as a symbol of Red Bull Racing’s commitment to redefining the future of Formula 1 technology.
