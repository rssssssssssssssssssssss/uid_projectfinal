Red Bull RB16

Specifications

Production Years 2021
Engine 1.6L V6 Turbo Hybrid
Power Approx. 950 HP
Transmission 8-speed sequential gearbox
Features Advanced energy recovery, aerodynamic innovations, lightweight chassis

Overview

The Red Bull RB16 epitomizes Red Bull Racing’s relentless pursuit of performance during the 2021 Formula 1 season. Its engineering excellence and aerodynamic finesse enable unrivaled speed on the track.

History & Development

Developed specifically for the high demands of 2021, the RB16 integrated cutting‑edge hybrid components and state‑of‑the‑art chassis design, marking a crucial evolution for the team.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Featuring a meticulously engineered lightweight chassis, the RB16’s aerodynamic package minimizes drag while maximizing downforce, essential for high‐speed cornering.

Engine & Hybrid Powertrain

Powered by a 1.6L V6 turbo hybrid engine, the RB16 makes the most of its energy recovery systems, delivering both explosive power and excellent efficiency.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Every component of the RB16 is precision‑crafted by Red Bull Racing engineers, embodying the team’s commitment to excellence and innovative design.

Mileage & Efficiency

Though built for speed, the RB16’s advanced hybrid technology ensures optimal fuel usage and energy management across race distances.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Enhanced safety features and telemetry‑driven driver assistance systems guarantee that the RB16 not only performs superbly but also operates with high safety standards.

Additional Information

The RB16’s revolutionary design and performance have set a new benchmark in Formula 1 technology, inspiring continuous innovation within Red Bull Racing.
