Maruti Suzuki Grand Vitara

Specifications

Production Years 1998 - Present
Engine Options 1.5L Petrol, 1.6L Diesel
Power 103 - 118 HP
Transmission 5-speed Manual, 6-speed Automatic
Features All-wheel drive, modern infotainment system

Overview

The Grand Vitara is a versatile compact SUV that blends practicality with style and performance. It offers a rugged yet refined experience for both urban commuting and off-road adventures.

History

Launched in 1998, the Grand Vitara has evolved with multiple generations that incorporate new technology and design updates, maintaining its status as a reliable and modern SUV.

Chassis & Design

Featuring a unibody construction and a bold, rugged design, the Grand Vitara offers a spacious interior combined with sturdy exterior styling, ideal for diverse driving conditions.

Engine & Performance

With petrol and diesel engine options, the Grand Vitara delivers adequate power and fuel efficiency, tailored to meet the demands of both city driving and off-road performance.

Manufacturing & Global Impact

Manufactured by Maruti Suzuki, the Grand Vitara has garnered worldwide appeal for its blend of durability, comfort, and modern features, contributing to the brand’s strong global presence.

Mileage & Efficiency

Engineered for efficiency, the Grand Vitara provides competitive mileage figures without compromising on performance, making it a cost-effective choice for diverse users.

Safety Features

Equipped with modern safety features including multiple airbags, ABS, and electronic stability control, the Grand Vitara meets international safety standards.

Additional Information

The Grand Vitara also boasts advanced connectivity, infotainment, and comfort features, making it a well-rounded vehicle for family use and adventure.
