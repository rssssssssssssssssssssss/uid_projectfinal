Mercedes-AMG One

Specifications

Production Years 2022 - Present
Engine 1.6L V6 Hybrid Turbocharged
Power Over 1000 HP
Transmission 7-speed automated manual
Features Active aerodynamics, hybrid technology, carbon fiber body

Overview

The Mercedes-AMG One is a revolutionary hypercar that seamlessly combines Formula 1 technology with a road-legal design. It delivers blistering performance, innovative hybrid power, and an unmistakable presence on the road.

History & Development

Developed in the early 2020s, the AMG One is the result of decades of racing expertise. Drawing on advanced F1 hybrid technology, it pushes the boundaries of automotive performance and represents a new era for high-performance road cars.

Chassis & Aerodynamics

Constructed with a lightweight carbon-fiber monocoque, the AMG One features active aerodynamics that adapt in real-time. This technology provides optimal downforce and minimal drag, ensuring exceptional handling at high speeds.

Engine & Hybrid Powertrain

At the heart of the AMG One is a 1.6L V6 hybrid turbo engine that produces over 1000 horsepower. This state-of-the-art powertrain combines the raw power of a combustion engine with the efficiency of an electric hybrid system, delivering both speed and sustainability.

Manufacturing & Assembly

Produced in limited numbers using bespoke manufacturing techniques, the AMG One is crafted with extreme precision. Every unit is built to meet the highest standards of performance and luxury, ensuring exclusivity and unrivaled quality.

Mileage & Efficiency

Despite its extreme performance, the AMG One’s hybrid technology allows for improved fuel efficiency relative to traditional hypercars. It strikes a balance between breathtaking acceleration and responsible energy consumption.

Safety & Driver Assistance

Equipped with advanced stability control, adaptive braking systems, and a host of modern safety features, the AMG One ensures that cutting-edge performance is never at the expense of driver safety and comfort.

Additional Information

The interior of the AMG One blends high-tech interfaces with premium materials. From its digital cockpit to its luxurious seating, every detail is designed to enhance the driving experience and provide an immersive environment for the driver.
