Hindustan Contessa

Specifications

Production Years 1984 - 2002
Engine 1.8L BMC / Isuzu Petrol/Diesel
Power 75 - 88 HP
Transmission 5-speed manual
Features Spacious interior, luxury features for its time

Overview

The Hindustan Contessa is a mid-size luxury sedan that brought a touch of elegance and comfort to Indian roads. Its refined design and performance made it a symbol of aspiration in its era.

History

Produced from 1984 to 2002, the Contessa was one of the few luxury sedans available in India, incorporating international design cues and refined engineering for its time.

Chassis & Design

Built on a robust body-on-frame chassis, the Contessa offered a spacious cabin and an exterior design that blended practicality with a sophisticated presence on the road.

Engine & Performance

With a 1.8L engine available in both petrol and diesel forms, the Contessa was engineered to provide smooth and reliable performance, prioritizing comfort and efficiency.

Manufacturing & Impact

Manufactured by Hindustan Motors, the Contessa became an aspirational car in India, representing luxury and exclusivity during its production years.

Mileage & Efficiency

The Contessa offered competitive mileage, making it a practical choice for long journeys and daily commuting despite its premium positioning.

Safety Features

Though basic compared to modern standards, the Contessa’s construction provided a reliable level of safety through sturdy build quality and passive safety features.

Additional Information

The Contessa’s spacious interior and luxurious appointments set it apart in its time, maintaining a legacy that is remembered fondly by automotive enthusiasts in India.
