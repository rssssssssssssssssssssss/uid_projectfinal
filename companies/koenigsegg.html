Koenigsegg - Road Cars

Koenigsegg is a Swedish hypercar manufacturer pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering with innovative technology, record-breaking performance, and breathtaking design.

Founded in 1994, Koenigsegg has earned its reputation by delivering exclusive, limited-production hypercars that challenge conventional engineering and redefine performance. Every Koenigsegg model is a blend of advanced aerodynamics, lightweight construction, and unrivaled power.

With models that showcase extreme speeds and meticulous craftsmanship, Koenigsegg’s vehicles are celebrated by automotive enthusiasts around the world.
