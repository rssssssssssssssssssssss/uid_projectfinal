Ferrari - Road Cars

Ferrari, founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939, is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer renowned for its iconic design, cutting-edge technology, and motorsport success. Headquartered in Maranello, Italy, Ferrari has created some of the most powerful and beautiful cars in automotive history.

Ferrari is known for its high-performance road cars that blend style, speed, and engineering excellence. Each model represents a perfect balance between aerodynamics, luxury, and raw power. Ferrari’s legacy is deeply tied to Formula 1, where it holds the record for the most Constructors’ Championships.

Beyond their performance, Ferrari vehicles are a status symbol, attracting car enthusiasts, collectors, and celebrities worldwide. Every Ferrari car is a masterpiece, combining Italian craftsmanship with state-of-the-art engineering.
